About us

We are a non-profit organisation set on supporting a platform for which people can express their concerns and opinions on products sold across Australia. We believe in fair and critical reviews and their power they hold for both consumers and producers. Please only say what needs to be said and help keep this site a positive place.

Our mission: We believe that no bad product should be sold twice, that's why we made it our mission to prevent unsatisfied customers around Australia with this website
